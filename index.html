The Papi Recipes

Lomo Saltado

The Lomo Saltado is a popular dish, that is a stir fry combine with sirloin, onions, tomatoes, and french fries. It is typically served with white rice.

Carne Asada

Carne asada is grilled and sliced beef, usually chuck steak, though skirt steak, flap steak, or flank steak can also be used. It is usually marinated then grilled or seared to impart a charred flavor.

Bistec Encebollado

Bistec encebollado is a traditional Puerto Rican recipe featuring cube steak marinated in a flavor-packed combo of onions, sofrito and vinegar, before being stewed in a savory tomato based sauce.

Molo de Pollo

Mole de pollo is dish that is typically made with stewed chicken smothered in a flavorful sauce. The sauce is made from a combination of chilies, nuts, tomatoes, spices, and sometimes chocolate.
